THE STORY

A Pulitzer Prize team photojournalist who dove to capture the first photos of the Nuestra Señora de Atocha treasure ship, photographed four Presidents and the Pope, survived being shot at during riots, and lived in a trailer documenting the aftermath of Hurricane Andrew.

With 25+ years as Senior Professor of Graphic Design at Broward College, Rick holds an MFA in Graphic Design, MA in Photography, and BA in Political Science from the University of Florida. He has published over 50 books, created 100+ copyrighted musical works, and completed 3,000+ dives in 120+ countries.

Now, as CEO of Imagine a World to the Power of AI, LLC, he channels a lifetime of creative firepower into 18 ecosystems — spanning AI education, marine science, disaster relief, political thrillers, children's stories, and a musical opera.
